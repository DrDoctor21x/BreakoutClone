How to play:

Destroy all of the bricks by bouncing the ball into them. The darker bricks need to be hit more than once.

If the ball touches the bottom side of the play space, you lose a life. If you lose all of them, the game ends.

To move the paddle either use the mouse or left and right arrow keys. You can control the ball's trajectory by either.

If you wish to save your score write your name in the box and click play! Otherwise, exit this window.
